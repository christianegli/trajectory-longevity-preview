What's new

Major updates to Trajectory — new features, new posts, data refreshes. Subscribe via RSS for ongoing research updates.

2026-05-07 feature

Compare protocols side by side

Pick 2–4 interventions on /compare/ and see them in a single attribute table — tier, evidence, scores, dose, who-should-consider, who-should-skip. URL-hash state means you can share a comparison.

  • Open the comparison view
2026-05-07 feature

Stack templates

Five curated example stacks for common situations — foundation, over-50 strength, cardiovascular, cognitive, metabolic — each with the rationale behind every protocol pick. A faster entry point than the wizard if your situation matches one of them.

  • Browse the stack templates
2026-05-07 seo

JSON-LD structured data + sitemap.xml

Per-page meta descriptions, a root sitemap covering 144 URLs, robots.txt, JSON-LD on every protocol, post, and the methodology page (FAQ schema). Search engines can now render rich results.

2026-05-07 feature

Site-wide search

A search box in the site header now searches protocols, posts, and concerns in real time. Keyboard Enter follows the top match. Built on a small client-side index.

2026-05-07 feature

News topic pages

Every tag in any post frontmatter now has its own /news/tag/<tag>/ page. The news index has a 'Browse by topic' section listing all tags with post counts.

2026-05-07 feature

Smoking and alcohol questions in the wizard

A new step asks about smoking and alcohol before generating your stack. Smoking-current outranks every other recommendation by an order of magnitude. Heavy alcohol is its own leverage point.

  • Take the quest
2026-05-07 feature

Stack scoreboard

After the wizard generates your stack, a KPI strip at the top shows total actions, daily/weekly cadence, estimated monthly cost range, doctor-conversation count, and the foundation/targeted/frontier mix.

2026-05-06 feature

Save / share / re-do diff

Stacks are saved to localStorage. A 'Welcome back' panel surfaces your last stack when you return. The Share button copies a URL with your answers compressed in the hash. After a re-do, a 'What changed since…' panel diffs added/removed/unchanged items.

  • Take the quest
2026-05-06 feature

Live stack refinement

On the wizard results page, every recommendation card now has Remove / Pin / Already-doing / Swap controls. Sticky control bar lets you change appetite and budget. Stack re-ranks instantly with the same deterministic engine.

2026-05-06 feature

Weekly schedule

The wizard now translates your generated stack into a 7×4 grid (days × time slots) — when to take what, when to train, when to fast. Print stylesheet collapses controls so it fits on one page.

2026-05-06 feature

Transparent reasoning on every recommendation

Each card on the results page now exposes the rules that fired (e.g. 'You sleep <6h', 'Goal: brain & memory') and the priority score behind its rank, with a link to the full evidence.

2026-05-06 content

Daily news / blog section

Trajectory now has a daily-ish news section at /news/. Posts are Markdown with frontmatter, cross-linked to protocols via [[protocol:slug]] shortcodes, and surfaced as 'Recent research' on protocol and concern pages. Subscribe via RSS.
