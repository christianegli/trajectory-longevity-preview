Over-50 Strength Stack

The anti-sarcopenia protocol — independence at 80 starts now.

For whom

Adults 50+ whose primary concern is staying physically capable. Anyone with frailty in the family, a parent who lost mobility early, or a self-assessment of "my strength is sliding."

What it replaces

Aimless gym time. Wandering supplement protocols.

Summary

After 50, the rate of muscle loss accelerates and "average" trajectories end in disability. The combined evidence for resistance training + protein + creatine in older adults is among the strongest in geriatric medicine. This stack is exactly that combination, with the supporting infrastructure that makes it sustainable.

Roughly $25–60/month. ~3 hours/week of training. The function-preservation move with the largest effect size available to you.

The stack

1
Resistance Training
T1Foundation $30–80/mo Ev A E 7 · C 9

The on-switch. 2× weekly, 4 compound movements (squat, hinge, push, pull), 3 sets of 6–10 reps with progressive overload.

2
Higher Protein Intake (1.2-1.6 g/kg)
T1Foundation $20–40/mo Ev A E 4 · C 8

Anabolic resistance after 50 means you need 1.2–1.6 g/kg/day, with 25–40g per meal to trigger MPS. The RDA is wrong for your goals.

3
Creatine
T1Foundation $10–25/mo Ev A E 5 · C 9

5g monohydrate daily. Best single supplement for older-adult muscle and probably cognition — concentrated benefit in vegetarians and short sleepers.

4
Vitamin D3
T1Foundation $3–10/mo Ev A E 4 · C 8

Falls and fracture prevention is where post-VITAL evidence still holds firmly, especially with osteoporosis context.

5
VO2max Training
T1Foundation $0–50/mo Ev A E 9 · C 9

Strength alone won't preserve cardiovascular capacity. One 4×4 session weekly raises the ceiling.

6
Sleep (7-9h, regularity, deep+REM)
T1Foundation $0 Ev A E 8 · C 8

Sleep deficits gut recovery and muscle protein synthesis. 7.5+ hours is part of the protocol, not separate from it.

7
Omega-3 (EPA/DHA)
T1Foundation $15–40/mo Ev A E 5 · C 7

Anti-inflammatory adjunct; modest evidence for sarcopenia attenuation when combined with resistance training.

8
Vitamin K2 (MK-7, MK-4)
T2Targeted $8–20/mo Ev C E 4 · C 4

Bone health pairing with vitamin D. MK-7 has direct fracture-reduction evidence.
