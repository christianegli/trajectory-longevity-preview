Sleep + Recovery Stack

Cautious sleep support only after schedule, light, caffeine/alcohol timing, pain, and breathing risk are addressed.

Content policy

Content stays free. Trust is the moat. Commerce is downstream of the free assessment and never changes evidence grades, rankings, recommendations, contraindications, or what Trajectory says not to take.

Intended fit

Assessment emphasizes sleep quality or recovery and does not surface higher-caution medication, pregnancy, breathing, or complex medical flags.

Not a fit

Not for untreated sleep apnea symptoms, severe insomnia, sedative medication complexity, pregnancy, seizure disorders, or users who need medical evaluation first.

Summary

This is not a sedative bucket. Magnesium is conditional, glycine is cautious, and melatonin is not a default subscription ingredient.

Checkout remains gated until fulfillment, safety review, and evidence-fit standards are ready. This page is an evidence-gated preview until vendors, fulfillment, labeling, support, refund, and compliance are approved.

Included ingredient slots

1
Magnesium conditional slot
Ev BOptional

Why included: Intake support only if not already covered by Foundation; avoids duplicate magnesium dosing.

Who should skip or pause: Kidney disease or already high supplemental magnesium intake.

Interactions / contraindications: Separate timing from some antibiotics, thyroid medications, and bisphosphonates.

Food, training, or medical-first note: Sleep schedule and dietary sources come first.

2
Glycine
Ev COptional

Why included: Cautious sleep-quality support candidate; framed as optional and low-drama.

Who should skip or pause: Pregnancy or complex medical conditions unless clinician approves.

Interactions / contraindications: Review with clinician when medication burden or chronic illness is significant.

Food, training, or medical-first note: Sleep schedule, light exposure, and caffeine timing come first.

3
Melatonin situational tool
Ev BOptional

Why included: Short-term timing tool candidate, not nightly filler and not included by default.

Who should skip or pause: Pregnancy, seizure disorders, anticoagulants, immunosuppressants, sedatives, pediatric use without clinician.

Interactions / contraindications: Potential interaction concerns with sedatives, anticoagulants, and immunosuppressants.

Food, training, or medical-first note: Circadian routine and medical evaluation for apnea/insomnia symptoms matter more.

What is deliberately not included

Sedative herb blends, high-dose melatonin as filler, GABA claims without clear fit, and alcohol-adjacent relaxation positioning.

Contraindications / review first

Pause for pregnancy, seizure disorder, sedative use, anticoagulants, immunosuppressants, kidney disease, untreated apnea symptoms, chronic insomnia, or complex medical conditions.

Required disclaimer

Educational information only. Supplements do not replace food, exercise, sleep, screening, or medications. Discuss medication interactions, pregnancy, chronic illness, surgery, abnormal labs, and upcoming procedures with a clinician.
