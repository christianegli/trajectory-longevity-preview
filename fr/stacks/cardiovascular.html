Cardiovascular Protective Stack

For the heart-disease-runs-in-the-family situation.

For whom

Adults with a parent or sibling who had a heart attack or stroke before 65, borderline-or-high LDL, borderline-or-high BP, or just a serious interest in primary prevention.

What it replaces

Vague cardiovascular concern. Random fish-oil purchases.

Summary

The cardiovascular protocols are the most evidence-rich category in longevity. This stack combines what's free (lifestyle), what's cheap (a few supplements with real LDL or BP signals), and the conversation to have with your physician (statin, BP medication if relevant).

Most components are foundation-grade. The single biggest move here is usually a doctor conversation, not a supplement.

The stack

1
Zone 2 Cardio
T1Foundation $0 Ev A E 8 · C 9

Cardiovascular fitness is the strongest single mortality predictor available to you. 3× 30 min weekly captures most of the benefit.

2
VO2max Training
T1Foundation $0–50/mo Ev A E 9 · C 9

Adds the ceiling — one 4×4 session weekly. The most modifiable single CV mortality lever after the basics.

3
Mediterranean Diet
T1Foundation Net neutral Ev A E 7 · C 9

PREDIMED showed 28% reduction in CV events in 5 years. Dietary pattern with the best RCT evidence in cardiology.

4
Extra-Virgin Olive Oil (Polyphenols)
T2Targeted $20–50 Ev A E 6 · C 8

The fat that does the work in PREDIMED. 4+ tablespoons daily of high-polyphenol EVOO.

5
Walnuts and Tree Nuts
T1Foundation $15–30/mo Ev A E 4 · C 8

PREDIMED nut group — daily mixed nuts (28g) had the same 28% CV event reduction as the EVOO group.

6
Omega-3 (EPA/DHA)
T1Foundation $15–40/mo Ev A E 5 · C 7

Modest CV benefit at 1–2g EPA+DHA daily; meaningful for high-triglyceride or low-fish-intake users.

7
Oat β-Glucan (Oatmeal, Oat Bran)
T1Foundation $3–8/mo Ev A E 4 · C 9

FDA-approved heart-disease claim. 3g/day soluble fiber from oats lowers LDL 5–10%.

8
Psyllium Husk Fiber
T2Targeted $8–15/mo Ev A E 4 · C 9

If oats aren't enough: 5–15g/day of psyllium husk lowers LDL ~9% — the cheapest LDL-lowering supplement on this site.

9
Statins
T2Targeted $5–30/mo Ev A E 7 · C 10

If LDL/ApoB is high and you're not on one, this is the doctor conversation. Single best-validated longevity drug we have. Not for everyone — but the evidence-based path runs through asking.

10
ACE Inhibitors / ARBs
T2Targeted $5–20/mo Ev A E 6 · C 9

If BP is high and you're not on a medication. Uncontrolled hypertension is the single biggest stroke and dementia risk factor that's easily fixable.

11
Sleep (7-9h, regularity, deep+REM)
T1Foundation $0 Ev A E 8 · C 8

Sleep regularity has independent CV mortality signal — separable from total duration.

12
Low / No Alcohol
T2Targeted Net savings. Ev A E 6 · C 8

Alcohol hits BP, AFib risk, and sleep before any clinical threshold. ≤3 drinks/week is the lowest-risk practical level.
