The Foundation Stack

Foundational only when your context supports it.

For whom

Adults whose assessment context supports these basics; food, training, sleep, screening, and medication decisions may matter more.

What it replaces

Nothing — this is the floor under every other stack on this page.

Summary

Twelve interventions chosen because the evidence is strong, the cost is near zero, and the risk is essentially nil. Most of the mortality benefit available to you is in this list, not in any frontier supplement.

All Tier 1. Most are free or under $20/month.

The stack

1
No Smoking
T1Foundation Free Ev A E 10 · C 10

Single highest-impact intervention in human medicine. Outranks every other line on this site by an order of magnitude — for the people it applies to.

2
Sleep (7-9h, regularity, deep+REM)
T1Foundation $0 Ev A E 8 · C 8

7.5+ hours, fixed timing. Compounds with everything else; deficits silently degrade every other intervention.

3
VO2max Training
T1Foundation $0–50/mo Ev A E 9 · C 9

Highest-leverage trainable mortality predictor. One hard interval session weekly raises the ceiling.

4
Zone 2 Cardio
T1Foundation $0 Ev A E 8 · C 9

Builds the aerobic base that VO2max sits on. 3× weekly conversational pace.

5
Resistance Training
T1Foundation $30–80/mo Ev A E 7 · C 9

The single most modifiable mortality predictor over age 50. Twice a week is enough.

6
Higher Protein Intake (1.2-1.6 g/kg)
T1Foundation $20–40/mo Ev A E 4 · C 8

1.2–1.6 g/kg/day with 25–40g per meal. The fuel under resistance training.

7
Mediterranean Diet
T1Foundation Net neutral Ev A E 7 · C 9

PREDIMED-grade RCT evidence. The dietary pattern with the cleanest cardiovascular signal.

8
Walnuts and Tree Nuts
T1Foundation $15–30/mo Ev A E 4 · C 8

Daily handful of mixed nuts is one of the most consistent mortality signals in nutrition.

9
Oat β-Glucan (Oatmeal, Oat Bran)
T1Foundation $3–8/mo Ev A E 4 · C 9

Cheapest evidence-based food upgrade. FDA-approved heart-disease claim.

10
Vitamin D3
T1Foundation $3–10/mo Ev A E 4 · C 8

Cheap insurance against deficiency, especially for older adults and limited-sun environments.

11
Omega-3 (EPA/DHA)
T1Foundation $15–40/mo Ev A E 5 · C 7

1–2g EPA+DHA daily. Modest cardiovascular benefit; useful when fish intake is low.

12
Creatine
T1Foundation $10–25/mo Ev A E 5 · C 9

30 years of safety data. Best single supplement for muscle and probably cognition over 50.
